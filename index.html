Frequently Ask Question

 What is Caricaturebuff.com?

A company and a website.
I started providing caricature services for retail purposes. I decided to create a website dedicated to offering caricature illustrations to be printed on T-Shirts, photo paper in a frame, canvas, mugs, tumblers, invitation cards and many items for a more personalized unique gifts and souvenir items.

 What exactly is a caricature?

A representation, especially pictorial or literary, in which the subject's distinctive features or peculiarities are deliberately exaggerated to produce a comic or grotesque effect.

Caricatures can be drawn as more portrait-like or grotesquely exaggerated however, Caricaturebuff.Com strives for more customer friendly caricatures.

 How did you start drawing caricatures?

I enjoyed drawing cartoons and faces since I was a kid. The first caricatures I did are just for a hobby but then I started learning how to use Photoshop software and figured out how to use it as a medium to create drawings specially caricature drawings. And through the help of concerned people, I finally have bought my own digitizer pen display that makes my works more define.

 Did you have to go to school to learn this?

No. I am a graduate of Entrepreneurial Management non-related course to my work as an artist though it became applicable when I started making my hobby my own business. I started to learn doing from simply editing pictures using photoshop and then I became so interested in learning tricks in photoshop software specially its tools used in painting.

 What do you use to draw with?

I use Wacom bamboo digitizing pen and tablet.

 Do you work from photographs?

Most of my works are done from digital photographs that customers sent via email attachment together with their information about the theme.
