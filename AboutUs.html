Definition of Caricature:
[kar-i-kuh-cher, -choor]
noun

1. A picture, description, etc., ludicrously exaggerating
the peculiarities or defects of persons or things.

2. Caricatures are meant to be funny and entertaining. It describes a person using exaggeration of some striking characteristics or distinctive features and oversimplification of others which usually denotes a dramatic work, suggests outlandish mimicry and broad comedy.

However, Caricaturebuff.com provides super friendly descriptions of the person instead of exhibiting the grotesque features of the client. We make sure that the client will be happy and will not feel insulted.
